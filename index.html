Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here's everything you need to know about our cleaning services.

We clean homes, offices, and commercial spaces of all sizes across Ghana. Whether you have a small apartment, a large family home, an office, or a commercial building, we have the expertise and equipment to handle it. We service Accra, Kumasi, Tema, Takoradi, and surrounding areas.

We offer next-day service or same-day cleaning, depending on availability. Book today, and in most cases, we can have your space cleaned tomorrow! For urgent requests, contact us directly and we'll do our best to accommodate your schedule.

Our standard cleaning includes all surfaces, floors, bathrooms, and kitchens. We dust, vacuum, mop, sanitize bathrooms, clean kitchen appliances and countertops, and ensure every corner of your space is spotless. Custom cleaning packages are also available based on your specific needs.

Yes! We use only certified eco-friendly cleaning products that are safe for you, your family, your pets, and the environment. Our products are effective at removing dirt and germs while being gentle and non-toxic.

Your satisfaction is our top priority. If you're not completely happy with our service, let us know within 24 hours and we'll come back to re-clean the areas of concern for free. We guarantee 100% satisfaction on every job.

Our pricing varies based on the size of your space, type of cleaning needed, and frequency of service. We offer transparent, competitive pricing with no hidden fees. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your specific needs. First-time customers get 20% off!
